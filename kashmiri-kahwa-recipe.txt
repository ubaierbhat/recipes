Ingredients:
 - 2 green cardamoms
 - 2 small (1 inch) sticks of cinnamon
 - 4/5 almonds (peeled)
 - 2/4 teaspoon sugar
 - 3 cups of water
 - 1 teaspoon green/kashmiri tea leaves(optinal)
 - 1/2 strands of saffron (optional)

Directions:
  1. Boil all (except almonds) the ingredients togather for 8 minutes (the colour will turn orange)
  2. Put some almonds in the cups and pour the kahwa into it.. 
  3. Enjoy

Other Information:
 - Kashmiri Kahwa is very helpful in cold weather and if you are not feeling well
